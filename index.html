Grammar Tips

  • Use active voice for clearer and more engaging writing.
  • Avoid run-on sentences by using proper punctuation.
  • Understand the difference between "their," "there," and "they're."
  • Use commas to separate items in a list and to clarify meaning.
  • Keep your sentences concise and to the point.
