A Moonlit Night (150 words)

A moonlit night is a beautiful sight. The moon shines brightly, making the land look silver. Everything feels peaceful and calm. The cool wind blows, and the stars twinkle in the sky.

The moonlight makes rivers shine like silver. Trees stand tall, their shadows stretching over the ground. The air feels fresh, and the world looks quiet and magical.

People love moonlit nights. Children play outside, running after fireflies. Older people sit and talk, remembering old times. Families gather and share happy moments under the moonlight.

Couples enjoy moonlit nights too. They walk together, feeling happy in the soft light. The beauty of the moonlight makes them feel closer.

A moonlit night is peaceful and lovely. It fills the heart with joy and makes everything look special.

A Book Fair You Visited (150 words)

Last week, I visited a book fair with my friends. It was held in a large open field. Many people came to see the fair. There were colorful stalls filled with books of all kinds. I saw books on stories, science, history, and more.

I bought a few books on adventure and science. The sellers were very friendly and helped me choose good books. There were also food stalls, and we enjoyed some snacks. Many students, teachers, and parents were at the fair. Everyone was happy to be around so many books.

The fair was a great place for book lovers. It helped people find good books at a low price. I enjoyed my time there and learned many new things. I hope to visit the book fair again next year.

A Book Fair You Visited (250 words)

Last Sunday, I visited a book fair with my best friend. It was held in a big field near our town. The fair was decorated with colorful banners and lights. Many people, including students, teachers, and parents, came to visit. The stalls were filled with books of different kinds. There were books on stories, science, history, travel, and even comics. I felt happy to see so many books in one place.

I visited many stalls and looked at different books. The booksellers were very friendly and suggested good books to buy. I bought some adventure and science books at a discount. There were also food stalls where we had some tasty snacks. People were reading books and discussing their favorite writers. A few writers were present at the fair, signing books for their readers. I also saw a storytelling session where a famous author read from their book.

The book fair was a great experience. It helped people find good books at low prices. It also encouraged young people to read more. Visiting the fair made me realize how important books are in our lives. Books give us knowledge, ideas, and inspiration. I felt very happy and learned many new things. I enjoyed my visit to the book fair and hope to visit again next year. It was a memorable day full of learning and fun.
